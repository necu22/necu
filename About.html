The history of the Necu company began in 2018. The founders, energetic and creative guys, brothers from a small town in the western part of Ukraine, decided to focus their activities on the development of Ukrainian streetwear culture. In the autumn of the same year, the first release was a parka called Necu. She became the first swallow from a wide range of models developed and implemented by the company. Ukrainians supported the youth brand and welcomed new product launches.

In 2020, the collections of the Necu brand already included both outerwear and shoes. The company's credo is constant movement forward. With each release, we set ourselves new tasks and work for maximum results.

Choosing Necu, you first of all get quality at an affordable price. We value each of our customers, we are proud of the large number of positive reviews. Product development and production technologies are constantly being improved - our customers deserve the best.
